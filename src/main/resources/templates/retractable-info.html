The retractable towbar is the luxury version of towbars. With the simple push of a button it becomes available – no matter if you want to go on tour with your cycle carrier or if you want to go on vacation with your caravan.

IT'S GENIUS: The towball can become invisible without having to be removed and stowed away. So you can never lose it!

THE BENEFITS OF A RETRACTABLE TOWBAR

  • Compact: The compact construction and special retractable mechanism make these towbars compatible even for newCars with limited available space behind the bumper.
  • Convenience: The plug on the ball neck makes it easy to wire the trailer or bike carrier into the newCar's electrical system.
  • Immediately ready for use & almost invisible:: The towbar releases in seconds when needed — the rest of the time it is hidden and stored behind the bumper.
